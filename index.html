GitHub Repository

General Introduction

This project explores plant disease classification using deep learning, with transfer learning strategies evaluated through VGG16 and ResNet50 models on a small dataset.

Dataset Overview

  • Source: Kaggle
  • Images: 1,530 (3 classes: Healthy, Powdery, Rust)
  • Pre-split into train/val/test

Transfer Learning Experiments

VGG16 Strategies

  • Strategy 1: Full training – accuracy: 96.67%
  • Strategy 2: Partial freezing – accuracy: 98.00% ✅ Best
  • Strategy 3: Fully frozen – accuracy: 87.33%

Partial freezing provided the best tradeoff between overfitting and underfitting.

Comparative Analysis: VGG16 vs ResNet50

VGG16: 98% accuracy – better for small datasets

ResNet50: 62% accuracy – too complex for this case

Data Augmentation

Augmentation (rotations, zoom, shifts) improved accuracy by 2.67%, made training more stable, and reduced overfitting.

Custom CNN: Pitfalls of Accuracy

Despite 92% test accuracy, F1-scores were poor due to class imbalance. Highlights need to use confusion matrix, not just accuracy.
